The Triangle of Disease

Understanding the Root Causes of Chronic Health Issues

What is the Triangle of Disease?

The Triangle of Disease concept identifies three key physiological systems that form the foundation of your health. When these systems are compromised, they create a "perfect storm" for chronic disease development.

1

Digestive System

Your digestive system is the gateway to health. When compromised, it affects nutrient absorption and allows toxins to enter the bloodstream.

2

Blood Sugar Regulation

Unstable blood sugar creates a cascade of hormonal imbalances, energy fluctuations, and cellular stress throughout the body.

3

Adrenal-Thyroid Axis

These critical hormone-producing glands regulate metabolism, stress response, and energy production at the cellular level.

Health Strategies

Addressing the Triangle of Disease requires a comprehensive approach to health:

  • Targeted nutritional support for each point of the Triangle
  • Lifestyle modifications to reduce system stress
  • Specific supplementation to address deficiencies
  • Regular assessment to track improvements
